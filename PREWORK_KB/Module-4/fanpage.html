Frank Ocean, a Beautiful Enigma

Frank Ocean is one of the most talented artists in the world right now. Whether you want a songwriter, a singer, a rapper, or all three Frank is your guy. I could speak at length about why that is, but I think the easiest way to qualify his greatness is to say that he got both Jennifer Hudson and Beyonce to perform uncredited voacl features on back to back albums!

Brief Musical Bio

Frank Ocean was born in Long Beach, California, on October 28, 1987. He grew up in New Orleans, but moved to Los Angeles in his late teens to pursue a career in music. Ocean started out ghostwriting tracks for such pop stars as Justin Bieber and Beyonc√©, but soon achieved fame as an artist when, in 2011, he released a mixtape of his own music, titled nostalgia, ULTRA. His next effort, channel ORANGE, was unveiled in 2012 to both critical and popular acclaim, and he followed with the back-to-back releases of Endless and Blonde in 2016.
